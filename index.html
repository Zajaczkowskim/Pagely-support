Frequently asked questions

How do I add a book?
Tap the “+” button, start typing the title, and pick a match — Pagely fills in the author and page count for you. You can also enter everything manually.

How do I update my current page?
Open a book you're reading and tap “Update progress,” or use the button on its card on the Reading tab. When you reach the last page, the book is marked finished.

How do I add books I want to read later?
When adding a book, set its status to “Want to Read.” It'll live on your wishlist in the Library tab until you start it.

Where is my data stored?
Everything stays on your device. Pagely has no account and no sign-up, and your library is never uploaded to us. When you search for a book title, only that text is sent to the public Open Library service to find matches.

Can I switch between light and dark mode?
Yes — tap the gear icon and choose System, Light, or Dark under Appearance.

How do I edit or delete a book?
Open the book, tap the menu (•••), and choose Edit or Delete.

Does Pagely work offline?
Yes. The only feature that uses the internet is the book-title search. Everything else works with no connection.

Privacy

Pagely does not collect personal data. Your reading library is stored locally on your device. Read the full Privacy Policy.
